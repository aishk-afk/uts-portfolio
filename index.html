Activity 1
Eastern & Western Perspectives
Images
This activity was looking into the influences of my eastern and western self. According to our discussion, Eastern Self is more connected with the community, our identity is one with the community. While our Western Self would take much pride from the successes.

As an Asian is it normal for us to be humble of our key successes.

As an Asian it is expected of us to honor our family and community.

Being influenced by the westerners, being prideful of where I have gone so far is not so bad.

Activity 3
Anthropological and Sociological Perspective
Images
This activity named "Looking-glass self". This idea is about a person's self-concept that is shaped by how they believe others see and judge them which is introduced by Charles Horton Cooley.

I have learned that how I perceive myself is not far from how others see me.

The descriptions of my friends about me are accurate to who I really am.

Activity 4
Physical and Sexual Self
Images
This made me reflect on my physical self, how I take care of myself physically, how I respect my body and honor it as well. My sexual self remains to what the Lord God has assigned me at birth. I remain to be a heterosexual and I identify myself as a female.

We should never judge a person based off their gender or gender identity.

Someone's gender does not affect their work performance.

The gender of a person does not define their worth as a person, their future, or their limitations.

Activity 5
Material and Digital Self
Images
I believe we Filipinos are sentimental about something in our lives. My tita would get really sentimental of all the things my grandmother and grandfather left for her. As I grow older, I come to understand the sentimental value of some things in our life. Personally, I value my gadgets more than other material stuff, because of the pictures or memories I have stored on my phone. I may not be socially active on social media, or posting updates regularly because I mainly use it for messaging, I post my memories privately, only I can see the updates. Especially in our time now, where identity thefts and other attacks are common it is our responsibility to safeguard our identity online.

There is no need to update the whole world of your status in life.
